Filipino-based Graffiti from Google Street-View for Training Dataset

by Reynaldo R. Isaac Jr.

This training dataset is designed to enhance the geographical generalization of graffiti image detection models, specifically focusing on the diverse visual landscape of the Philippines. Graffiti styles, materials, and cultural contexts can vary significantly across different regions, even within a single country. This dataset aims to capture this variability, enabling models to accurately identify graffiti in a wider range of Philippine locations and ultimately improving their robustness and real-world applicability.
