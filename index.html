Filipino-based Graffiti from Google Street-View for Training Dataset

by Reynaldo R. Isaac Jr.

×

This training dataset is designed to enhance the geographical generalization of graffiti image detection models, specifically focusing on the diverse visual landscape of the Philippines. Graffiti styles, materials, and cultural contexts can vary significantly across different regions, even within a single country. This dataset aims to capture this variability, enabling models to accurately identify graffiti in a wider range of Philippine locations and ultimately improving their robustness and real-world applicability. To achieve this, the dataset includes images from a variety of locations.

This dataset contains a total of 1,064 images of Filipino graffiti sourced from various cities across the Philippines. By compiling diverse graffiti examples, it aims to enhance the geographical generalization of detection models, allowing for more accurate identification in different urban contexts.
